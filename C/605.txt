    <h2 class="sectionTitle-heading">How much space do I need for a backup?</h2>
    <p class="sectionTitle-intro">To see the size of a specific file or folder, click it once and then press Command-I. To see storage information about your Mac, click the Apple menu in the top-left of your screen. Choose About This Mac and click the Storage tab.
