          <p><i>Learn to quickly set up your Cloudflare account and activate your domain. Understand best practices for Cloudflare-proxied domains. This article formerly appeared under the title Step 1: How does Cloudflare work?</i></p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="7shaTtP1eu8p86sKRWrowh">Overview</h2>
          <p>Cloudflare’s global network enhances website security and performance. Review our article on&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/205177068">how Cloudflare works</a>&nbsp;for further details.</p>
          <p>Activate your domain on Cloudflare in two steps:</p>
          <ol>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/201720164">Create a Cloudflare account and add a website</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/205195708">Use Cloudflare nameservers at your domain registrar</a></li>
          </ol>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           Step 2 is not required for Business and Enterprise domains on <a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/360020348832">CNAME setups</a>. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p>Additionally, Cloudflare recommends several&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/360027989951-Getting-Started-with-Cloudflare#h_87dac948-9e12-47e0-a32c-2144aadd0a2a">best practices</a>&nbsp;for newly activated domains.&nbsp; If you run into issues setting up your first Cloudflare domain, refer to our&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360027710672">Troubleshooting FAQ for new Cloudflare customers</a>.</p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="1FLTAjfZWsFOvkN3t458T2">Best practices for active Cloudflare domains</h2>
          <p>Cloudflare recommends the following actions and enhancements for each new domain added to Cloudflare:</p>
          <h3><b>Recommended actions</b></h3>
          <p>Ensure successful interaction between your origin web server, third-party services, and Cloudflare:</p>
          <ul>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/201897700">Allow Cloudflare IP addresses</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/217074967">Allow third-party services</a><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/217074967"></a></li>
          </ul>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           Your origin server IPs are automatically allowed for your domain when adding the domain to Cloudflare. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <h3><b>Recommended enhancements</b></h3>
          <p>Learn which Cloudflare features improve your site speed and optimize security:</p>
          <ul>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200172856#h_c2f90417-ee71-4b65-b6c7-3636ee2b3c63">Improve site performance</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/115002059131">Optimize security</a></li>
          </ul>
          <h3><b>Recommended reading</b></h3>
          <p>Secure traffic over HTTPS for both your site visitors and for traffic between Cloudflare and your origin web server.</p>
          <ul>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360024787372">End-to-end HTTPS with Cloudflare</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360027710672">Troubleshooting FAQ for new Cloudflare customers</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200172476">Contacting Cloudflare support</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200172676">Understanding Cloudflare DDOS protection</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200170166">Best Practices: DDoS preventative measures</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360021801151">Getting started with DNS</a></li>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360023792171">Getting started with Cloudflare SSL</a></li>
