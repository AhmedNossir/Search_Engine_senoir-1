      <p>The <b>WikiProject Council</b> is a group of Wikipedians that encourage and assist with the development of WikiProjects. The council acts as a central point for inter-WikiProject discussion and collaboration. New to WikiProjects? See <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_Signpost/2013-04-01/WikiProject_report" title="Wikipedia:Wikipedia Signpost/2013-04-01/WikiProject report">FAQs about WikiProjects</a> for the answers to the most common inquiries. The <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Guide" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Guide">WikiProject guideline page</a> outlines generally accepted protocols and conventions. </p>
      <p><br> </p> 
      <h2><span id="What_do_we_do.3F"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="What_do_we_do?">What do we do?</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: What do we do?">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <ol>
       <li>Document best practices for WikiProject structure and operation; see the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Guide" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Guide">WikiProject Guide</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Guide/Task_forces" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Guide/Task forces">Task Force Guide</a>.</li> 
       <li>Provide a <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject_Council" title="Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Council">central forum</a> for discussing issues of interest to a wide range of WikiProjects.</li> 
       <li>Assist in the formation of new WikiProjects and revitalization of dormant WikiProjects. We manage the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Proposals" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Proposals">WikiProject proposals</a> page and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Guide#Tools_for_WikiProjects" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Guide">catalog tools</a> available for building and maintaining WikiProjects. If you need help getting started, just ask at the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject_Council" title="Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Council">talk page</a>!</li> 
       <li>Maintain a <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Directory" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Directory">directory of WikiProjects</a> to help interested editors find communities with similar interests.</li>
      </ol> 
      <h2><span id="How_can_you_help.3F"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="How_can_you_help?">How can you help?</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: How can you help?">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2> 
      <p>There's no formal list of participants here, so there's no need to sign up. To help out, just monitor the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:WikiProject_Council" title="Wikipedia talk:WikiProject Council">talk page</a> and join ongoing discussions! You can also help to update the manual <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Council/Directory" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Council/Directory">WikiProject Directory</a> by checking to see if section listings are up-to-date. </p>

      <p><br> <b>More on WikiProjects</b> </p> 
      <ul>

They maintain several pages on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Content_assessment" title="Wikipedia:Content assessment">the assessment system</a> supporting <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Version_1.0_Editorial_Team/Work_via_WikiProjects" title="Wikipedia:Version 1.0 Editorial Team/Work via WikiProjects">WikiProjects' work</a>.</li> 
       <li>A <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_Signpost/2013-04-01/WikiProject_report" title="Wikipedia:Wikipedia Signpost/2013-04-01/WikiProject report">2013 article</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Signpost" title="The Signpost">The Signpost</a></i> by <a href="/wiki/User:Mabeenot" title="User:Mabeenot">Mabeenot</a> answers various WikiProject frequently asked questions. <i>The Signpost</i> also has a now-irregular <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_Signpost/Newsroom/Content_guidance#WikiProject_report" title="Wikipedia:Wikipedia Signpost/Newsroom/Content guidance">WikiProject report</a>. To contribute, go to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_Signpost/Newsroom" title="Wikipedia:Wikipedia Signpost/Newsroom"><i>The Signpost</i> Newsroom</a>.</li> 
       <li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Requests_for_comment" title="Wikipedia:Requests for comment">RfCs</a> related to WikiProjects are listed <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Requests_for_comment/WikiProjects_and_collaborations" title="Wikipedia:Requests for comment/WikiProjects and collaborations">here</a>.</li>
      </ul>
