        <p>GUADEC sponsorship offers a variety of benefits, and encourages sponsors to become more deeply involved in the community:</p> 
        <div class="section group"> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img src="../wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/4.jpg" alt="">
          <br> <strong>Meet key contributors</strong>
          <br> GUADEC is a great opportunity to meet contributors and users of one of the leading free and open source technology platforms.
         </div> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img class="alignnone" src="http://2015.guadec.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/3.jpg" alt="" width="1000" height="750">
          <br> <strong>Influence product direction</strong>
          <br> Sponsoring and attending GUADEC not only gives you advance information on GNOME’s plans for the coming year, but provides you a voice in shaping them.
         </div> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img src="../wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/8.jpg" alt="">
          <br> <strong>Access technical talent</strong>
          <br> Experienced developers from around the globe attend GUADEC. Meet business partners who can help you achieve your technical goals.
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="section group"> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img src="../wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/6.jpg" alt="">
          <br> <strong>Encourage technology adoption</strong>
          <br> GUADEC has been a site for product launches, and a great place to encourage developers to use new technologies.
         </div> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img src="../wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/9445513292_04018d0557_k.jpg" alt="">
          <br> <strong>Contribute to the development of GNOME technologies</strong>
          <br> Help the development of new features and components. GUADEC hosts planning and coding sessions, as well as technology and interoperability meetings.
         </div> 
         <div class="col span_1_of_3">
          <img src="../wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2014/10/9443651048_5f9fef1b4d_k1.jpg" alt="">
          <br> <strong>Give back to free and open source software</strong>
          <br> You’ll be recognized not only as a sponsor of GUADEC, but also as a supporter of free software, the GNOME project, and the ideals on which these are founded.
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <p>More details can be found in the <a href="../wp-content/uploads/2016/04/GUADEC-2016-Brochure.pdf">GUADEC 2016 Sponsorship Brochure</a> and the <a href="../wp-content/uploads/2014/10/conference-brochure-2016-web.pdf">2016 Conference Sponsorship Brochure</a>.</p> 
        <p>If you’re interested in sponsoring GUADEC in 2016, email us at <strong><a href="mailto:sponsors@guadec.org">sponsors@guadec.org</a></strong>.</p>
