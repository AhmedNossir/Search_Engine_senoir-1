          <p><i>Learn to set up your Cloudflare account and activate your domain by watching short video tutorials.</i></p>
          <div class="cf-toc">
           <ul>
            <li><a href="#1Al8BL3K2cq56r0baGDJ4u">Overview</a></li>
            <li><a href="#24ZFznWWFk3vwaxeMGmlFc">Add a domain to Cloudflare</a></li>

            <li><a href="#4UFBdCRZNniEV2xsnQHpqt">Add MX records to Cloudflare</a></li>
            <li><a href="#6dEVd998jdo06Kv0kZUYiK">Performance overview</a></li>
            <li><a href="#60TYAhsj31sVZZV4FlPa5j">Security overview</a></li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="1Al8BL3K2cq56r0baGDJ4u">Overview</h2>
          <p>Getting started with Cloudflare is a multi-step process. The following video tutorials guide you through the onboarding process and highlight some best practices.</p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="24ZFznWWFk3vwaxeMGmlFc">Add a domain to Cloudflare</h2>
          <p>When you create a new Cloudflare account, part of the process includes <a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/201720164"><u>adding a new domain</u></a>.</p>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/1434cf5af367af0385e4c54d8300ea59" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p> 
          <p></p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="66zv5PsLZLwoJRnOlssKPK">Change domain nameservers to Cloudflare</h2>
          <p>To use Cloudflare as your authoritative DNS provider, you must <a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/205195708-Changing-your-domain-nameservers-to-Cloudflare"><u>update the nameservers</u></a> at your domain registrar so that your web traffic routes through the Cloudflare network. This video explains how to change your nameservers at your domain registrar when adding your website to Cloudflare.</p>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/d29bdc08f20edcb9f7ec8223f893bb21" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p>
          <p></p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="4UFBdCRZNniEV2xsnQHpqt">Add MX records to Cloudflare</h2>
          <p>Adding DNS records to Cloudflare is required during the onboarding process. Cloudflare DNS only proxies HTTP traffic, so MX records can’t be proxied through our network. This video demonstrates best practices when <a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/200168876-Email-undeliverable-when-using-Cloudflare"><u>adding MX records to Cloudflare</u></a>.</p>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/787115d7926481486d49e361d25e9cf6" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p>
          <p></p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="6dEVd998jdo06Kv0kZUYiK">Performance overview</h2>
          <p>Cloudflare creates features that improve your site performance. This video describes key performance-enhancing features, including Auto-Minify, Brotli, Polish, and WebP.</p>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           Some of these features require at least a Pro plan to activate. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/33c5906bb4c591856b66f66db2df6019" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p>
          <p></p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="60TYAhsj31sVZZV4FlPa5j">Security overview</h2>
          <p>Cloudflare offers many solutions to encrypt web traffic. This video describes the basics of HTTP and HTTPs, and demonstrates how to secure and protect your site using the Cloudflare SSL/TLS tab.&nbsp;</p>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/d9ed966c74c7b367db155b5c01f6b56c" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p>
          <p></p>
