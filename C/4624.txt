        <h2 class="margin-top0">Why GitLab for GitOps?</h2> 
        <p> Foster collaboration between your infrastructure, operations, and development teams. Deploy more frequently with greater confidence while also increasing the stability, reliability, and security of your software environments. GitLab gives you version control, code review, and CI/CD in a single application for a seemless experience. Tight integrations with HashiCorp Terraform and Vault along with multi-cloud capabilities provide you with the best platform for infrastructure automation. </p> <a href="/demo/" class="btn cta-btn orange margin-top20">Learn more</a>

        <h2>What is GitOps?</h2> 
        <p> <strong>GitOps</strong> is an operational framework that takes DevOps best practices used for application development such as version control, collaboration, compliance, and CI/CD, and applies them to infrastructure automation. </p> <a href="/topics/gitops/" class="btn cta-btn orange margin-top20">Learn more</a>

        <h2 class="margin-top0">GitLab advantages</h2> 
        <ul> 
         <li> <strong>A single app for SCM and CI/CD</strong> Source Code Management and CI/CD are at the core of GitOps. A single app with a seamless experience keeps you productive. </li> 
         <li> <strong>Tightly integrated with Terraform</strong> Terraform has emerges as the industry standard for environment provisioning. GitLab <a href="/partners/technology-partners/hashicorp/">partners with HashiCorp</a> to make sure your tooling works the best together. </li> 
         <li> <strong>Trusted by the world's largest engineering teams</strong> From Goldman Sachs and Verizon to Ticketmaster and Siemens, more large enterprise trust GitLab with their code than anyone else. </li>
