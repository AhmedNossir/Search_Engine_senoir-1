       <h1 class="glue-mod-text-center glue-headline glue-headline--headline-1 hero-text orphan-joiner" aria-label="Building a safer, more trusted internet.">Building a safer,<span><br> more trusted internet.</span></h1> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="glue-grid glue-mod-spacer-3-top"> 
      <div class="glue-grid__col glue-grid__col--span-0-sm glue-grid__col--span-2-md glue-grid__col--span-3-lg"></div> 
      <div class="subcopy glue-headline glue-grid__col--span-4-sm glue-headline--headline-5 glue-mod-text-center
          glue-grid__col glue-grid__col--span-8-md glue-grid__col--span-6-lg orphan-joiner"> 
       <p>Today, we have Google teams working across privacy, security, content responsibility and family safety around the world. Most of this Internet safety work happens in our Google Safety Engineering Centres in Munich and Dublin, led by experienced teams of engineers, policy specialists and subject matter experts. By understanding the problem, developing solutions, partnering with others and empowering users everywhere, we can build a better, safer Internet for all.</p>
