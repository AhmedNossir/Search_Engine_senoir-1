      <p><b>Special pages</b> are pages that are created by the software on demand. They are located in their own <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Help:Namespaces" title="Special:MyLanguage/Help:Namespaces">namespace</a> (<i>Special</i>) and are not editable directly as other pages. </p>
      <p>Some special pages depend on the <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Help:Preferences" title="Special:MyLanguage/Help:Preferences">preferences</a> that have been set by a user, e.g. the number of titles which is displayed on a user's <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Help:Watchlist" title="Special:MyLanguage/Help:Watchlist">watchlist</a>. </p> 
      <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="List_of_special_pages">List of special pages</span></h2> 
      <p>Clicking the link <i><a href="/wiki/Special:SpecialPages" title="Special:SpecialPages">SpecialPages</a></i> will take you to a list of almost all special pages on a wiki. Such a link is often accessible in the <a href="/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Help:Navigation#Toolbox" title="Special:MyLanguage/Help:Navigation">toolbox</a> on the left hand panel.
