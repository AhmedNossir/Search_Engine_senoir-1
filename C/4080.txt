       <h1>About TTB</h1> 
       <p>The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is a bureau under the <a href="https://www.treasury.gov/Pages/default.aspx">Department of the Treasury</a>. We employ staff across the country, including our Headquarters Offices in Washington, D.C., and the <a href="/offices/national-revenue-center" target="_self">National Revenue Center</a> in Cincinnati, Ohio.</p> 
       <p>Our staff are highly educated and technically trained; more than half are analysts, chemists, investigators and auditors. In addition, a large number of employees serve as financial, legal, information management, and computer specialists. See how we are <a href="/who-we-are/about-us/organizational-structure" target="_self">organized</a>.</p> 
       <h2>Our Mission</h2> 
       <p>Our mission is to collect Federal excise taxes on alcohol, tobacco, firearms, and ammunition and to assure compliance with Federal tobacco permitting and alcohol permitting, labeling, and marketing requirements to protect consumers.</p>
