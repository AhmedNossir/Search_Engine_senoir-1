      <p><b>Did you know</b>... is a section you can see on the Main Page. Beginners should consult <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Glossary" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Glossary">this Did You Know glossary</a> if they don't understand any of the terms used. Beginners may like to start by looking at a summary of our <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Onepage" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Onepage">many rules</a>: this provides links to pages with more detailed explanations (which in turn may link to even more detailed pages). </p>
      <p>Here are the ways you can help. The ways are listed in increasing order of the Did You Know experience that is required to do these jobs well. So beginners should choose from the top of the list. Statistics show most people who read this page don't click anything, but that wasn't the intent. Please choose from the list and read on; there's more. </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>You can help <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Proofreading" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Proofreading">proofread nominations</a>.</li> 
       <li>You can <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Article" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Article">write a new article</a>. But you might want to study the nomination process first or write your article in <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:User_space" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:User space">user space</a>, or your article might be too old before it's ready.</li> 
       <li>You can <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Nomination" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Nomination">nominate a new article and write a Did You Know hook for it</a>.</li> 
       <li>You can <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Approval" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Approval">approve or disapprove nominations</a>.</li> 
       <li>You can <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Preparation_areas" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Preparation areas">promote nominations</a> to the preparation areas.</li> 
       <li>You can help <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYKADMINBOT" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYKADMINBOT">our software</a> to schedule regular moves from the preparation areas to the Main Page, or <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Guide" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Guide">do it the old way</a> if the software isn't working.</li>
      </ul>
