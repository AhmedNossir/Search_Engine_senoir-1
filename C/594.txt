   <div class="sectionTitle   "> 
    <h2 class="sectionTitle-heading">Upgrade to macOS Big Sur</h2>
    <p class="sectionTitle-intro">Before installing this upgrade, make sure that your Mac is ready.</p>
   </div> 
   <div class="contentLink contentBlock "> 
    <div class="u-layout u-layout--3up"> 
     <div class="u-layout-item"> 
      <div class="contentLink-item"> 
       <div class="contentLink-block"> 
        <h3 class="contentLink-title" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block" data-analytics-link-component_name="default-contentblock">Check compatibility</h3>
        <div class="richText"> 
         <div class="table-responsive"> 
          <div class="contentLink-copy" data-analytics-event="link.click" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block">
           <p>macOS Big Sur supports most Mac models introduced in 2013 and later.</p>
          </div>
         </div> 
        </div> <a class="contentLink-link" data-analytics-event="link.click" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block" data-analytics-link-component_name="View compatible Mac models" data-analytics-link-url="/en-us/ht211238" href="/en-us/ht211238"><span class="contentLink-text">View compatible Mac models</span><span class="icon icon-chevronright" aria-hidden="true"></span></a>
       </div> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="u-layout-item"> 
      <div class="contentLink-item"> 
       <div class="contentLink-block"> 
        <h3 class="contentLink-title" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block" data-analytics-link-component_name="default-contentblock">Make a backup</h3>
        <div class="richText"> 
         <div class="table-responsive"> 
          <div class="contentLink-copy" data-analytics-event="link.click" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block">
           <p>Before you upgrade, make a complete backup with Time Machine, or store your files in iCloud.</p>
          </div>
         </div> 
        </div> <a class="contentLink-link" data-analytics-event="link.click" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block" data-analytics-link-component_name="Learn how to back up your Mac" data-analytics-link-url="/mac-backup" href="/mac-backup"><span class="contentLink-text">Learn how to back up your Mac</span><span class="icon icon-chevronright" aria-hidden="true"></span></a>
       </div> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="u-layout-item"> 
      <div class="contentLink-item"> 
       <div class="contentLink-block"> 
        <h3 class="contentLink-title" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block" data-analytics-link-component_name="default-contentblock">Free up space</h3>
        <div class="richText"> 
         <div class="table-responsive"> 
          <div class="contentLink-copy" data-analytics-event="link.click" data-analytics-link-component_type="Content Block">
           <p>If the installer needs more storage space to upgrade, you can free up space using tools that come with your Mac.</p>
