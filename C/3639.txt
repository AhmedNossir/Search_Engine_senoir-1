   <section id="faq" class="section" aria-label="Frequently asked questions" data-module="faq-01"> 
    <div class="row"> 
     <div class="column medium-3"> 
      <h2>Frequently asked questions</h2> 
      <div class="row row-size4 column"> 
       <ul class="list--links"> 
        <li> <a href="https://status.azure.com/" data-bi-slot="1 ">Current operational status</a> </li> 
        <li> <a href="/en-us/global-infrastructure/services/?products=azure-applied-ai-services" data-bi-slot="2 ">Product availability by region</a> </li> 
       </ul> 
      </div> 
     </div> 
     <div class="column medium-8 medium-offset-1"> 
      <ul class="faq"> 
       <li class="faq__item"> <button type="button" class="faq__question" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="2c884d43" data-bi-slot="1" data-bi-id="faq-1" data-bi-an="faq-01"> Where are Applied AI Services available? </button> 
        <div class="faq__answer" id="2c884d43">
         Availability of Azure Applied AI Services varies by service. See the page for the service you are interested for availability details.
        </div> </li> 
       <li class="faq__item"> <button type="button" class="faq__question" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="5398e019" data-bi-slot="2" data-bi-id="faq-2" data-bi-an="faq-01"> What is the service-level agreement (SLA) for Applied AI Services? </button> 
        <div class="faq__answer" id="5398e019">
         <a href="https://aka.ms/AppliedAISLA">Visit our SLA page to learn more</a> about SLAs for Applied AI Services.
        </div> </li> 
       <li class="faq__item"> <button type="button" class="faq__question" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="e4eeb8c2" data-bi-slot="3" data-bi-id="faq-3" data-bi-an="faq-01"> How do I get started with Applied AI Services? </button> 
        <div class="faq__answer" id="e4eeb8c2">
         <p>Applied AI Services are immediately available in the Azure portal. You do not need to go through a partner to access these services from Azure. However, if you prefer, there are partners with experience in your industry who are also ready to help. </p>
         <p>To learn more about how to get started on your own or how to connect with a partner, visit the page of the Applied AI Service that you’re interested in.</p>
        </div> </li> 
       <li class="faq__item"> <button type="button" class="faq__question" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="fe8ad35e" data-bi-slot="4" data-bi-id="faq-4" data-bi-an="faq-01"> What is the difference between Applied AI Services and Cognitive Services? </button> 
        <div class="faq__answer" id="fe8ad35e">
         <p>Both Applied AI Services and Cognitive Services are designed to help developers create intelligent apps. Cognitive Services provides general purpose AI services that serve as the core engine for Applied AI Services. </p>
         <p>Applied AI Services build on top of Cognitive Services with additional task-specific AI and business logic to optimize for specific use cases so that developers spend less time designing solutions or setting up pipelines. </p>
         <p>If there isn't an Applied AI Service available to meet to meet a specific use case, developers can also build their own solutions from scratch, using Cognitive Services as building blocks.</p>
        </div> </li> 
       <li class="faq__item"> <button type="button" class="faq__question" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="66d20737" data-bi-slot="5" data-bi-id="faq-5" data-bi-an="faq-01"> Does Microsoft offer AI services for customers who aren't developers or data scientists? </button> 
        <div class="faq__answer" id="66d20737">
         Yes. Domain experts who want to build AI apps in a low-code environment can use <a href="https://powerapps.microsoft.com/">Microsoft Power Apps</a>, which can be implemented without developer or data science expertise.
        </div> </li> 
      </ul>
