           <ul>
            <li><a href="#3gFGymd3N8sYzvnqA0VxTR">Overview</a></li>
            <li><a href="#6NswogCXqM6TSaxqEf5Bz4">Create a Cloudflare account</a></li>
            <li><a href="#2YulMb5YJTVnMxgAgNWdS2">Add a domain to Cloudflare</a></li>
            <li><a href="#1n0A0a2FtqBIHwo1t4Wmek">Related resources</a></li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="3gFGymd3N8sYzvnqA0VxTR">Overview</h2>
          <p>To experience the benefits of Cloudflare, first&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/201720164#h_6a9066c4-9dbc-4c0d-821e-155f9401900a">create a Cloudflare account</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/201720164#h_8ea0d664-3af3-4623-ae2d-052f98b10090">add a domain to Cloudflare</a>. When you create a new Cloudflare account, adding an initial domain is included in the workflow.</p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="6NswogCXqM6TSaxqEf5Bz4">Create a Cloudflare account</h2>
          <ol>
           <li>Visit&nbsp;<a href="https://dash.cloudflare.com/sign-up">https://dash.cloudflare.com/sign-up</a>.</li>
           <li>Enter your&nbsp;<b>Email&nbsp;</b>address and&nbsp;<b>Password</b>.</li>
           <li>Click<b>&nbsp;Create Account.</b></li>
          </ol>
          <p><b></b></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-tip">
           Use an email alias or distribution list (i.e. <em><a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#8eede2e1fbeae8e2effcebceebf6efe3fee2eba0ede1e3"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="a5c6c9cad0c1c3c9c4d7c0e5c0ddc4c8d5c9c08bc6cac8">[email&nbsp;protected]</span></a></em>) This email alias is the main point of contact for Cloudflare billing and service-related email notifications. Ensure that the email alias can both send and receive emails for account recovery and troubleshooting. 
          </div><b></b>
          <p></p>
          <p>4. The Cloudflare UI asks you to&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/en-us/articles/201720164#h_8ea0d664-3af3-4623-ae2d-052f98b10090">add a site to Cloudflare</a>.</p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="2YulMb5YJTVnMxgAgNWdS2">Add a domain to Cloudflare</h2>
          <p></p>
          <p class="cf-video-container"> <iframe style="border:none" src="https://watch.cloudflarestream.com/1434cf5af367af0385e4c54d8300ea59" width="480" height="320" allowfullscreen></iframe> </p>
          <p></p>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           If adding a new domain while in the workflow of creating a new account, skip to step 3 below. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p>1. Log in to your Cloudflare account.</p>
          <p>2. Click on&nbsp;<b>Add site </b>from the top navigation bar.</p>
          <p>3. Enter your website’s root domain and then click <b>Add Site</b>. For example, if your website is <i>www.example.com</i>, type <i>example.com</i>.</p>
          <p>4. Cloudflare attempts to automatically identify your DNS records. This process takes approximately 60 seconds to complete.</p>
          <p>5. Click <b>Next</b>.</p>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           You may see the remaining Enterprise domain quota, if applicable. Contact your Cloudflare Account Team if you have reached your Enterprise domain quota. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p>6. <a href="https://www.cloudflare.com/plans/#compare-features">Select a plan level</a>.</p>
          <p>7. Click <b>Confirm</b>&nbsp;in the <b>Confirm Plan </b>window that appears.</p>
          <p>8. Review whether all DNS records were identified in the <b>DNS query results</b>&nbsp;window.</p>
          <ul>
           <li><a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/360019093151">Manually add missing DNS records</a></li>
           <li>Decide&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200169626">which subdomains enable Cloudflare</a>&nbsp;security and performance features&nbsp;or bypass Cloudflare.security and performance features&nbsp;or bypass Cloudflare</li>
          </ul>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           Some records, like MX, cannot proxy through Cloudflare and don’t display a Cloud icon. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p>9. Click <b>Continue</b>.</p>
          <p>10. Copy the 2 Cloudflare nameservers displayed and click&nbsp;<b>Continue.</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
          <p></p>
          <div class="cf-sidebar-2 cf-note">
           Once assigned, Cloudflare Support cannot move your domain to a different nameserver or a different Cloudflare IP address. 
          </div>
          <p></p>
          <p>11. To finish domain setup and activate your domain on Cloudflare,&nbsp;<a href="https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/205195708">change your domain nameservers to Cloudflare</a>.</p>
          <hr>
          <h2 id="1n0A0a2FtqBIHwo1t4Wmek">Related resources</h2>

          </ul>
