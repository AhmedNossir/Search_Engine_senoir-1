      <h1 class="hero__title"> <span class="hero__title-text">About YouTube</span> <img src="https://www.gstatic.com/youtube/img/branding/youtubelogo/svg/youtubelogo.svg" alt="YouTube"> </h1> 
      <h2 class="hero__mission-intro js-header-sub-logo lb-font--no-crop">Our mission is to give everyone a voice and show them the world.</h2> 
      <p class="hero__mission">We believe that everyone deserves to have a voice, and that the world is a better place when we listen, share and build community through our stories.</p> 
     </div> 
    </section>
    <section class="freedoms"> 
     <div class="freedoms__inner js-freedom-ribbons"> 
      <h2 class="freedoms__section-title">Our values are based on four essential freedoms that define who we are.</h2> 
      <div class="freedoms__item js-parallaxed" data-speed="0.03"> 
       <h3 class="freedoms__title lb-font--no-crop">Freedom of <span class="freedoms__ribbon js-freedom-ribbon">Expression</span></h3> 
       <p class="freedoms__copy lb-font--no-crop">We believe people should be able to speak freely, share opinions, foster open dialogue, and that creative freedom leads to new voices, formats and possibilities.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="freedoms__item js-parallaxed" data-speed="0.08"> 
       <h3 class="freedoms__title lb-font--no-crop">Freedom of <span class="freedoms__ribbon js-freedom-ribbon">Information</span></h3> 
       <p class="freedoms__copy lb-font--no-crop">We believe everyone should have easy, open access to information and that video is a powerful force for education, building understanding, and documenting world events, big and small.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="freedoms__item js-parallaxed" data-speed="0.03"> 
       <h3 class="freedoms__title lb-font--no-crop">Freedom of <span class="freedoms__ribbon js-freedom-ribbon">Opportunity</span></h3> 
       <p class="freedoms__copy lb-font--no-crop">We believe everyone should have a chance to be discovered, build a business and succeed on their own terms, and that people—not gatekeepers—decide what’s popular.</p> 
      </div> 
      <div class="freedoms__item js-parallaxed" data-speed="0.08"> 
       <h3 class="freedoms__title lb-font--no-crop">Freedom to <span class="freedoms__ribbon js-freedom-ribbon">Belong</span></h3> 
       <p class="freedoms__copy lb-font--no-crop">We believe everyone should be able to find communities of support, break down barriers, transcend borders and come together around shared interests and passions.</p> 
      </div> 
     </div>
    </section>
