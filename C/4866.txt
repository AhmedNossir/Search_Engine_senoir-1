         <td class="mbox-text">The pages in this category are <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Redirect" title="Wikipedia:Redirect">redirects</a></b> from pages that are <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:TPROT" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:TPROT">template-protected</a> from editing for any of several possible reasons, or (infrequently) from <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Subject_page" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Subject page">subject pages</a> that are template-protected as an intermediate protection level between <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:SPP" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:SPP">semi-</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:FPP" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:FPP">full-page</a> protection. For more information follow the links. <i>Never <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Substitution" title="Wikipedia:Substitution">substitute</a></i> redirect template(s), nor place them on <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Soft_redirect" title="Wikipedia:Soft redirect">soft redirects</a>.<br><small>See also the <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_messages/Redirect_pages" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Template messages/Redirect pages">complete list of redirect templates</a></b> and the <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:WikiProject_Redirect/Style_guide" title="Wikipedia:WikiProject Redirect/Style guide">redirect style guide</a></b>.</small></td>

      <p>This category contains <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Redirect" title="Wikipedia:Redirect">redirects</a> that have been <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Protection_policy" title="Wikipedia:Protection policy">template-protected</a>. Template-protected redirects are constructed for the purpose of preventing <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Vandalism" title="Wikipedia:Vandalism">vandalism</a> or <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Edit_war" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Edit war">edit warring</a>, but may be a plausible template shortcut, as an alternative to <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Protected_deleted_pages" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Protected deleted pages">deleting the page</a>. Template protection prevents a template page from being edited except by <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Administrators" title="Wikipedia:Administrators">administrators</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Template_editors" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:Template editors">template editors</a>. </p>
      <p>Populating this category does not in itself protect the page. Actual page protection is engaged by administrators. </p>
