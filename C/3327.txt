         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">With so much uncertainty and concern surrounding the recent novel coronavirus, it’s easy to feel helpless—but there is something we can all do to help the fight against it. Aside from keeping safe and </span><a href="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/php/risk-assessment.html"><span style="font-weight: 400;">following guidelines from medical experts</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, we can all do our part to get help to people and regions that have been affected.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">By fundraising for coronavirus relief efforts, or donating to a </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/f/covid19-relief-fund"><span style="font-weight: 400;">COVID-19 relief fund</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, we can all do our part and provide much-needed support and aid. With the growing number of quarantined areas, individuals in these areas need vital medical help and day-to-day items to pull through this new threat.</span></p> 
         <p style="text-align: center;"><span class="button button-wp large primary expanded-mobile"><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/sign-up">Start a coronavirus fundraiser</a></span></p> 
         <h2><span class="heading-2">What is the coronavirus and how can you stay safe?</span></h2> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The only thing that’s certain at this point about the coronavirus (COVID-19) is that it’s a respiratory infection that can be transmitted from person to person. Aside from this, scientists are still uncovering other factors about the disease and its spread. For this reason, it’s important to rely on the advice of medical experts and be wary of misinformation that is circulating, such as unproven cures and ways to fight the disease.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To get the </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">latest </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">news</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and facts on the</span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/coronavirus-facts"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> coronavirus</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, </span><a href="https://www.cdc.gov/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is a great place to start. As of today, the CDC has provided numerous resources with ever-evolving information about the disease, and even </span><a href="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/about/prevention-treatment.html"><span style="font-weight: 400;">steps to prevent the illness</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. It’s also good to remember basic tips on </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">personal</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> cleanliness that can reduce your risk of contracting the disease, such as washing your hands often, not touching your face, and</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> practicing </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">social</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> distancing</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you want to take added steps to prevent your risk of exposure, look to the </span><a href="https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/notices"><span style="font-weight: 400;">CDC’s travel notices</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, which include information about high-risk regions. Once you ensure you’re taking the necessary steps to reduce your own risk, consider helping others that may not have as many options.</span></p> 
         <h2><span class="heading-2">Why is it important to support those affected by COVID-19?</span></h2> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The impact of the coronavirus has extended far beyond those who have become infected. Entire communities now face emergency lockdowns, and in Italy alone, over </span><a href="https://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-51787238"><span style="font-weight: 400;">16 million people have been quarantined</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> in an attempt to prevent further spread of the disease.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">With such a staggering number of people in quarantine and more becoming sick, needs quickly mount. Everything from basics, like food to </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/emergency-financial-assistance">emergency financial assistance</a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to pay for things like rent is urgently needed as people experience lost wages due to illness or quarantine restrictions. For those who are already sick, </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/pay-medical-bills?lang=en-uk">paying medical bills</a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> has become an additional burden.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In countries like the United States, where not everyone may have access to insurance, financial help for the coronavirus is desperately needed to ensure people don’t avoid seeking treatment or testing because they don’t have insurance. However, even in countries with universal healthcare, the sheer surprise of this new threat has led to a huge push to raise money for coronavirus for growing demands for medical supplies and equipment.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <h2><span class="heading-2">Ways to provide coronavirus financial relief </span></h2> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As the threat of this disease grows and puts further strain on communities, here are a few things we can all consider doing to provide some relief:</span></p> 
         <h3><span class="heading-3">1. Directly support individuals impacted by the virus</span></h3> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As the crisis grows, so do the negative effects on peoples’ health and their financial wellbeing, but we can all do our part to help those affected. Here’s how making a direct donation to </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">a</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">COVID-19 fundraiser</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> can help:</span></p> 
         <p><strong>Living expenses</strong></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Hourly workers and people without paid sick time are among the most vulnerable to the negative financial effects of the pandemic. With no way to cover expenses, workers like these will inevitably fall behind on rent and other fixed expenses. For these reasons, it’s important to get ahead of the crisis and start providing support to individuals like these.</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Read </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/coronavirus-relief-homeowners"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Coronavirus Relief for Homeowners and Renters: A Resource Guide</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for additional resources.</span></p> 
         <p><strong>Food, water, and other basics</strong></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Low-income households are already strained in good times but this new threat makes it even more important to provide immediate financial assistance to these households.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For more help read </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/coronavirus-resources-single-parents"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Four Crucial Resources for Single Parents During the Coronavirus</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/coronavirus-relief-low-income-families"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Coronavirus Relief for Low Income Families: Ways to Find Support</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p> 
         <p><strong>Small business and unemployment support</strong></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Millions of </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/small-business-relief-fundraisers"><span style="font-weight: 400;">small businesses affected by the coronavirus</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> are now suffering severe economic hardship because of the outbreak. Starting a fundraiser for your favorite local restaurant, bar, coffee shop, or boutique is one way to help business owners as well as service industry workers. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">You can also help promote them on social </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">media</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> like Facebook or </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Twitter</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to attract more </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">donors</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to their existing fundraisers.</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> You can also donate to </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">these businesses yourself </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">to ensure they stay afloat through this crisis.</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are you a small business owner who is struggling to pay your bills? See our blog posts </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/coronavirus-relief-small-businesses"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Coronavirus Relief for Small Businesses: Seven Ways to Get Help </span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">and </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/fundraising-tips-for-small-businesses"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Best Fundraising Tips for Small Businesses</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Now would also be a good time to explore new strategies like setting up a </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">virtual team</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or using the power of digital marketing channels such as </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">email</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p> 
         <p><strong>Emergency medical treatment, medication, and equipment</strong></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As our economy becomes strained and more people are affected by financial pressures, the ability for people to pay for medication and treatment becomes a real hurdle to seeking treatment. As a result, people across GoFundMe have already started their COVID-19 fundraising efforts, and you can do your part to provide direct financial assistance to fundraisers, such as the examples included below.&nbsp;</span></p>

          <li>&nbsp;<a href="https://www.gofundme.com/f/xpcwgy-conquering-covid19">Conquering COVID-19</a></li>

         </ul> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">And these are just a few examples of what people are trying to do to help each other during this crisis. At GoFundMe, you can find many more great acts of kindness. To find more fundraisers like the ones above, check out our hub for COVID-19, </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/act/covid19">How to Help Those Affected by COVID-19</a>.</p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Donating directly to these </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">coronavirus fundraising</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">campaigns</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> may provide the immediate financial support families and individuals need to rise above this crisis. While donating to charities or communal fundraisers is also a great option, fundraisers for individuals may be the best way to guarantee people get the money they need to get immediate relief.</span></p> 
         <h3><span class="heading-3">2. Create care packages with supplies</span></h3> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">While everyone should be taking precautions, some people in our communities may be at a greater risk if they are elderly or have preexisting health conditions. For people who are not able to get around easily due to preexisting conditions, preparing for potential quarantines may be much harder. To help these individuals, consider creating care packages to help reduce their risk of exposure.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><a href="https://wgntv.com/news/coronavirus/volunteers-seek-donations-for-free-coronavirus-care-packages-delivered-to-seniors-in-need/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Volunteers in Chicago</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> did just this when they decided to create care packages for seniors. As the threat of COVID-19 grew, these volunteers took action and devised a plan to get supplies to people who need the most help during this time of crisis.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you’d like to do this on your own but don’t know where to start, consider reaching out to a local charity and ask if they would like to partner with you. Local charities may already be doing this, so it would be a great opportunity for you to volunteer your time with them. </span></p> 
         <h3><span class="heading-3">3. Start a crowdfunding coronavirus fundraiser for yourself or someone you know</span></h3> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Another great way to provide support is by creating a fundraiser for someone in need. By setting up a coronavirus relief fundraiser for someone that needs help, you can help them overcome potential hurdles they’re facing because of the spread of COVID-19. Setting up a coronavirus fundraiser is just one way to do this, but you can also look to other </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/top-crowdfunding-sites"><span style="font-weight: 400;">crowdfunding platforms</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and find the right fit.&nbsp;</span></p> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Of course, if you’re the person in need, you may want to start a coronavirus fundraiser for yourself. You may have a sick family member, have childcare needs, or even be out of work as a result of quarantine measures in your region. Even today, only a few months after the disease was discovered, you’ll find hundreds of COVID-19 fundraisers that people have created, and many are for themselves. If you’re in a quarantined region or need </span><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/c/blog/emergency-fund"><span style="font-weight: 400;">emergency funds</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to prepare, a </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">coronavirus donation</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">drive</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> could make all the difference.</span></p>

         <h3><span class="heading-3">4. Donate to coronavirus relief efforts</span></h3> 
         <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Another great way to help is by supporting organizations that are providing </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">COVID-19 relief funds</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to people and communities affected by the coronavirus.
