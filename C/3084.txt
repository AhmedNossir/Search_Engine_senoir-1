           <h2>When to Contact the FBI</h2>
           <p><img src="https://www.fbi.gov/image-repository/2012_hq_sioc-026.jpg/@@images/db1e0dcb-cd1b-4a0d-b24a-daf5158716f4.jpeg" data-val="fe9319f8bce8451fb4c3cd7feabcd513" data-scale="preview" alt="Inside a Strategic Information and Operations Center conference room with two telephones, a large screen, and several people." data-mce-src="resolveuid/fe9319f8bce8451fb4c3cd7feabcd513/@@images/image/preview" data-linktype="image" class="image-right" title="Telephone on Desk at FBI Headquarters">If you have an emergency that requires an immediate law enforcement response, please call 911 or contact your local Police Department or Sheriff’s Department first.</p>
           <p>The FBI is a federal investigative and intelligence agency with jurisdiction in a wide range of federal crimes; national security matters such as terrorism and espionage; cyber/computer crimes and intrusions; and intelligence activities that relate to those missions.</p>
           <p>Please contact your <a data-mce-href="resolveuid/6d82bb7e4c5d4e81824bf9edcd286b85" data-val="6d82bb7e4c5d4e81824bf9edcd286b85" data-urltype="_direct_" data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/contact-us/field-offices" title="Local FBI Field Office">local FBI office</a> or&nbsp;submit a tip electronically if you have information about:</p>
           <ul>
            <li>Possible acts of terrorism, including violence, funding, or recruitment;</li>
            <li>Persons sympathetic to terrorists or terrorist organizations;</li>
            <li>Suspicious activities that you believe threaten national security, especially suspicious activities that involve foreign powers or foreign organizations;</li>
            <li>Computer crimes or intrusions into computer networks, particularly those associated with national security;</li>
            <li>Corrupt activities in state, local, or federal governments or in law enforcement;</li>
            <li>Racial or hate crimes, human trafficking (involuntary servitude or slavery), or other civil rights crimes;</li>
            <li>Organized crime activities;</li>
            <li>Financial crimes that involve fraud, especially corporate fraud, mortgage fraud, or other investment fraud schemes where significant dollar losses have occurred, including those impacting you or your place of work;</li>
            <li>Fraud in the health care industry;</li>
            <li>Persons who have committed or are planning to commit bank robbery, kidnapping, extortion, or thefts of valuable art, large interstate shipments of goods, or monetary instruments; and activities of violent gangs.</li>
           </ul>
           <p>See <a data-mce-href="resolveuid/8338420b170b46f68601123311729bdc" data-val="8338420b170b46f68601123311729bdc" data-urltype="_direct_" data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/investigate" title="What We Investigate">What We Investigate</a> for more information on our areas of investigative responsibility. <br></p>

           <h2>Reporting Crime</h2>
           <p>You can report violations of U.S. federal law or submit information in a criminal or terrorism investigation as follows:</p>
           <ul>
            <li>Use our online <a data-urltype="_direct_" data-val="7519309f11b14e50ac712dddadd8ee88" href="https://www.fbi.gov/tips" data-linktype="internal" data-mce-href="resolveuid/7519309f11b14e50ac712dddadd8ee88">Tips and Public Leads form</a>;</li>
            <li>Contact <a data-urltype="_direct_" data-val="6d82bb7e4c5d4e81824bf9edcd286b85" href="https://www.fbi.gov/contact-us/field-offices" data-linktype="internal" data-mce-href="resolveuid/6d82bb7e4c5d4e81824bf9edcd286b85">your local FBI field office</a> or closest <a data-urltype="_direct_" data-val="4d0bdb98c17f4843ac02f7b677c74872" href="https://www.fbi.gov/contact-us/legal-attache-offices" data-linktype="internal" data-mce-href="resolveuid/4d0bdb98c17f4843ac02f7b677c74872">international office</a>;</li>
            <li>To report suspicious activity involving chemical, biological, or radiological materials, call (toll-free): 855-TELL-FBI&nbsp; or 855-835-5324.</li>
            <li>Report an online scam or e-mail hoax by filing a complaint online with our <a data-urltype="/view" data-val="http://www.ic3.gov" href="http://www.ic3.gov" data-linktype="external" data-mce-href="http://www.ic3.gov">Internet Crime Complaint Center</a> or by using our online Tips and Public Leads form;</li>
            <li>To provide information on select major cases, call our Major Case Contact Center at 1-800-CALL-FBI (225-5324)</li>
           </ul>
