       <p>The data transfer fees charged by many cloud providers can be an integral part of your cloud hosting bill.        <p>Our partners have agreed to pass on these cost savings to our joint customers by waiving or reducing data transfer charges.</p> 
       <p>To help you get a sense of the scale of your potential savings by moving to the Bandwidth Alliance, we have put together a calculator. </p>
