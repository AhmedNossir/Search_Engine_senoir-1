       <div class="ytr-article__content ytr-js-tabs-content-block lb-hide" id="overview"> 
        <p class="md md-p">Every day, people from around the world come to YouTube to experience the world’s biggest cultural moments. Whether hosting a live charity event, a town hall or a press conference about breaking news, YouTube Live and Premieres allow Creators to bring viewers together in real-time to learn, discuss and to form new social communities.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="ytr-article__content ytr-js-tabs-content-block lb-hide" id="youtube-live"> 
        <p class="md md-p">YouTube Live is an easy way for Creators to reach their community in real time. Whether streaming an event, teaching a class, or hosting a workshop, YouTube has tools that will help manage live streams and interact with viewers in real time.</p> 
        <p class="md md-p">Creators can live stream on YouTube via <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9228389?hl=en&amp;ref_topic=9257984" rel="noopener" target="_blank">webcam</a>, <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9228390?hl=en" rel="noopener" target="_blank">mobile</a>, and <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2907883?hl=en&amp;ref_topic=9257984" rel="noopener" target="_blank">encoder streaming</a>. Webcam and mobile are considered great options for beginners and allow Creators to go live quickly. Encoder streaming is ideal for more advanced live streams such as: sharing creator’s screen or broadcast your gameplay, connecting to external audio and video hardware, and managing an advanced live stream production (like multiple cameras and microphones).</p>

       <div class="ytr-article__content ytr-js-tabs-content-block lb-hide" id="live-chat-moderation-tools"> 
        <p class="md md-p">Moderating a channel’s live chat makes our YouTube community more welcoming and healthy. YouTube offers <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9826490?hl=en&amp;ref_topic=9257984" rel="noopener" target="_blank">live chat moderation tools</a> to help prevent harassment and make everyone feel safe. Some of these tools include: assigning moderators, blocking words list, holding inappropriate chats for review, slow mode, and turning off Live Chat. Before starting a live stream or debuting a Premiere on YouTube, we encourage everyone to familiarize themselves with our moderation tools and <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9288567?hl=en" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Community Guidelines</a> to keep live chat positive for viewers.</p> 
       </div> 
       <div class="ytr-article__content ytr-js-tabs-content-block lb-hide" id="monetization"> 
        <p class="md md-p">For Creators to monetize their live stream or Premiere, their channel needs to be part of the <a class="md md-a" href="https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/72851?hl=en" rel="noopener" target="_blank">YouTube Partner Program (YPP)</a>. Creators have several ways to earn money from live streams and Premieres: Ads, Super Chat and Super Stickers, and Channel Memberships.</p> 
        <p class="md md-p">*Feature availability varies by country and we are working to expand to more countries.</p>
