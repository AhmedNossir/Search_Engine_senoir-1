      <h1><span class="mw-headline" id="Wikimedia_Logo">Wikimedia Logo</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=User:Neolux&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Wikimedia Logo">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h1> 
      <p><a href="/wiki/File:Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg" class="image"><img alt="Wikimedia Foundation RGB logo with text.svg" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c4/Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg/200px-Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg.png" decoding="async" width="200" height="200" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c4/Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg/300px-Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c4/Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg/400px-Wikimedia_Foundation_RGB_logo_with_text.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="400" data-file-height="400"></a> </p>
      <p>In my original design, the colours are red, blue and green, represent the three primary colours of light, as well as the three colours that were used for links on MediaWiki projects at the time (The current default theme has changed the green links to a lighter shade of blue.) </p>
      <p>The white space in the main circular area forms a stylised "w" representing "wiki". This allows the green area to represent a book, which is a symbol of knowledge. The blue arc surrounding the book represents "encompassing" that knowledge, which is something that the Wikimedia projects hope to do. The smaller red circle interrupts the blue arc, showing the incomplete nature of a wiki. Completing the arc would imply that all knowledge has been encompassed, which is a practical impossibility. The combination of all these elements forms a humanoid, with the red circle being the head, the blue arc being arms and the book of knowledge being the heart/torso. </p>
      <p>The colours and typeface (Gill Sans) I chose for this design, were adopted as the official colours and typeface for Wikimedia according to the original <a class="external text" href="https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_visual_identity_guidelines/Archived">Wikimedia visual identity guidelines (Archived)</a>. Since then, the logo and <a class="external text" href="https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_visual_identity_guidelines">guidelines</a> have evolved to remove the colour, and change the typeface to the Open Font <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat">Montserrat</a>. </p>
      <p><a href="/wiki/File:Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg" class="image"><img alt="Wikimedia Foundation logo - vertical.svg" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg/200px-Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg.png" decoding="async" width="200" height="150" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg/300px-Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg/400px-Wikimedia_Foundation_logo_-_vertical.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="512" data-file-height="383"></a> </p>

      <h1><span class="mw-headline" id="WikiQuote_logo">WikiQuote logo</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=User:Neolux&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: WikiQuote logo">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h1> 
      <p><a href="/wiki/File:Wikiquote-logo-en.svg" class="image"><img alt="Wikiquote-logo-en.svg" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Wikiquote-logo-en.svg/200px-Wikiquote-logo-en.svg.png" decoding="async" width="200" height="200" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Wikiquote-logo-en.svg/300px-Wikiquote-logo-en.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Wikiquote-logo-en.svg/400px-Wikiquote-logo-en.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="150" data-file-height="150"></a> </p>
      <p>The design choice for this was to take elements from the design of the foundation logo, and re-purpose them. </p>
      <p>The gestalt of the logo is effectively a talking head. The arcs being stylised sound waves as the circular head says something. </p>
      <p>The typeface (<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.t26.com/fonts/display.php?f_id=352">Lassigue D Mato</a>) was chosen to be a script as a quote is a sometimes a quick quip, or something transitory, something this particular typeface epitomises. </p>
      <p>The fading in colour of the arcs mainly is a
