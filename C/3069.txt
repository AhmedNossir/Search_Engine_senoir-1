           <h3><a data-linktype="external" href="http://ucr.fbi.gov" data-val="http://ucr.fbi.gov" data-mce-href="http://ucr.fbi.gov" data-urltype="/view">Crime Statistics</a></h3>
           <p>Annual Uniform Crime Reporting statistical publications from data provided by thousands of law enforcement agencies across the United States.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-quarter col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/cjis/identity-history-summary-checks" data-val="77efcd67ba274da8ab81f2dc5e16c15d" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/77efcd67ba274da8ab81f2dc5e16c15d" data-urltype="_direct_">Identity History Summary Checks</a></h3>
           <p>Information taken from fingerprint submissions retained by the FBI in connection with arrests and, in some instances, federal employment, naturalization, or military service.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-half col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/cjis" data-val="183cf79552104db8bb746b66bd0fe68a" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/183cf79552104db8bb746b66bd0fe68a" data-urltype="_direct_">Criminal Justice Information Services</a></h3>
           <p>The world’s largest repository of criminal fingerprints, other biometrics, and history records for use by investigators, law enforcement officers,and other criminal justice professionals. <br></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-three-quarters col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/laboratory" data-val="0b54b1f2bea148f791f39a5d680dd4f8" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/0b54b1f2bea148f791f39a5d680dd4f8" data-urltype="_direct_">FBI Laboratory Services</a></h3>
           <p>Providing forensic exams, technical support, expert witness testimony, and advanced training to Bureau personnel and partners around the globe.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
       <div class="mosaic-grid-row row"> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-leftmost col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/information-management/name-checks" data-val="d984f801658345c48f444cd6d34e1d5a" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/d984f801658345c48f444cd6d34e1d5a" data-urltype="_direct_">Name Checks</a></h3>
           <p>Background information from FBI files for government employment or appointment, security clearances, attendance at a White House function, issuance of immigration benefits, naturalization, or a visa to visit the United States.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-quarter col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile mosaic-tile-padding-bottom"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/resources/law-enforcement/security-clearances-for-law-enforcement" data-val="695a1ce196994627953895de922cd6c0" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/695a1ce196994627953895de922cd6c0" data-urltype="_direct_">Security Clearances for Law Enforcement</a></h3>
           <p>State and local officials with an established "need to know" regarding classified information that would or could affect their area of jurisdiction must apply for a security clearance through their local FBI office.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-half col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/scams-and-safety/sex-offender-registry" data-val="f58bb9aa182744eb8f7254d8fae1e41a" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/f58bb9aa182744eb8f7254d8fae1e41a" data-urltype="_direct_">Sex Offender Registry</a></h3>
           <p>Search the latest information from all 50 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, Guam, and numerous Indian tribes for the identity and location of known sex offenders.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-quarter mosaic-position-three-quarters col-md-3"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/training-academy" data-val="03b3a299795646188f516de1354f87e2" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/03b3a299795646188f516de1354f87e2" data-urltype="_direct_">Training</a></h3>
           <p>The FBI Academy is dedicated to being the world’s premier law enforcement learning and research center and an advocate for law enforcement’s best practices worldwide.<br></p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div>

          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/file-repository/fd-961-for-internet.pdf/view" data-val="27e47e978f884c1b94093179d7e8ca8b" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/27e47e978f884c1b94093179d7e8ca8b/view" data-urltype="/view">Bioterrorism Security Risk Assessments</a></h3>
           <p>Forms and instructions for Bioterrorism Risk Assessments.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile mosaic-tile-padding-bottom"> 
          <div class="mosaic-tile-content"> 
           <h3><a target="_blank" href="https://www.fbi.gov/services/cjis/nics" data-linktype="internal" data-urltype="_direct_" data-val="aa76a017d7144ec890e09593c42ad592" data-mce-href="resolveuid/aa76a017d7144ec890e09593c42ad592">National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS)</a></h3>
           <p>NICS is used by Federal Firearms Licensees (FFLs) to instantly determine whether a prospective buyer is eligible to buy firearms or explosives.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-third mosaic-position-third col-md-4"> 
         <div class="movable removable mosaic-tile mosaic-plone.app.standardtiles.rawhtml-tile mosaic-tile-padding-top">

           <h3><a data-linktype="internal" href="https://www.fbi.gov/file-repository/identity-history-summary-request-fd-258-110120/view" data-val="d38ffbd5d46a43728b385c5db47e74fc" data-mce-href="../resolveuid/d38ffbd5d46a43728b385c5db47e74fc/view" data-urltype="/view">Standard Fingerprint Form (FD-258)</a></h3>
           <p>The FD-258 is the Applicant Fingerprint Card (white card with blue ink) most often used by law enforcement, government agencies, and banking institutions.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div>

           <p>Information on ordering fingerprint cards and training aids.</p> 
          </div> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
        <div class="mosaic-grid-cell mosaic-width-third mosaic-position-two-thirds col-md-4">

           <p>How to find FBI records already released and how to request unreleased records through the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) or the Privacy Act.</p>
